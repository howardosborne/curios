Sweden

Sweden, a Scandinavian country nestled in Northern Europe, boasts breathtaking natural beauty, a rich cultural heritage, and a progressive society. If you're looking for a destination that offers stunning landscapes, charming cities, and unique experiences, Sweden should be on your travel bucket list.
