Lithuania

Lithuania, a country steeped in history and natural beauty, is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. Located in the Baltic region of Northern Europe, Lithuania offers a captivating blend of medieval architecture, stunning coastlines, and lush green landscapes.

Places to visit in Lithuania

Why not take a look at these fab places to visit.

Kaunas

Lithuania's second-largest city and a cultural hub, known for its Art Nouveau architecture, Pažaislis Monastery, and Žalgiris Arena.

How to get to Lithuania flight-free

Getting to Lithuania and the other Baltic countries from western Europe isn’t quick, but it can be a great experience that will help you appreciate the importance of the Baltic to northern Europe.

My tip is to take the ferry from Kiel to Klaipeda, but there are many over land options which you can explore with Trip Hop
