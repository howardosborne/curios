Latvia

Latvia, a country on the Baltic Sea between Lithuania and Estonia, is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. While its capital, Riga, is a popular tourist destination, Latvia offers a captivating blend of historical charm, stunning natural beauty, and a vibrant cultural scene.

How to get to Latvia flight-free

Getting to Latvia and the other Baltic countries from western Europe isn’t quick, but it can be a great experience that will help you appreciate the importance of the Baltic to northern Europe.

My tip is to take the ferry from Travemunde to Liepāja, but there are many over land options which you can explore with Trip Hop
