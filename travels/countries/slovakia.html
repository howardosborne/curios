Places to visit in Slovakia

Why not take a look at these fab places to visit.

Banska Bystrica

A city in central Slovakia, known for its medieval Old Town, SNP Square, and the Matej Bel University.

more...
Bratislava

Slovakia's capital and largest city, known for its medieval Old Town, Bratislava Castle, and Devin Castle.

more...
Kosice

Slovakia's second-largest city, known for its medieval Old Town, St. Elisabeth's Cathedral, and Singing Fountain.

more...
Zilina

A city in northwestern Slovakia, known for its historic center, the Budatín Castle, and the Rajecké Teplice spa town.
