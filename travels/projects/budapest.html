Budapest

On This Page

Budapest, capital of Hungary and one of the great jewels in the Hapsburg Empire. Here are some strolls that provide a glimse into that Magyar nation and it’s hitory.

Strolls

Cemetery stroll 3.5km

This fascinating cemetery is a wander into Hungary’s past and offers a reflection on how a nation sees itself.

VII District 4.5km

This stroll takes in the distinct VII district of Budapest. Formerly the Jewish quarter, it still contains a synagogue, kosher cafes but overlaid with a modern independent streak.

Keleti to Nyugati 4km

Here’s a stroll between two principal stations in Hungary that takes in some of the genteel sites of Budapest, apart from the House of Terror.

Thermal baths and heroes 6km

This walk from Keleti takes in a great Hungarian tradition - thermal baths. The route also covers Heroes square and a series of galleries and museums.
