What are the chances of missing a connection?

There are plenty of stats on the punctuality of trains, but I’ve struggled to find anything that shows what the consequences of those late running trains, particularly in terms of missing connections are.

To help understand the problem better, I’ve made a live tracker showing arrivals into stations across Europe and for the late-running trains, what viable connections would be missed.
