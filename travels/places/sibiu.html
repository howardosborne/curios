Sibiu

Hermannstadt1191, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Another picturesque town in Transylvania, Sibiu was the European Capital of Culture in 2007 and has a rich cultural and architectural heritage. You can admire the colourful houses, the Brukenthal Museum, and the Bridge of Lies.
