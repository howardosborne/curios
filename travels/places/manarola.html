Manarola

Timothy A. Gonsalves. CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Manarola, a tiny village clinging to the cliffs along the Italian Riviera is a gem. It's the second-smallest of the five villages that make up Cinque Terre, yet it bursts with charm, colorful houses, and stunning coastal views. The only trouble is that this place has been 'found', so you'l be sharing it with all the other people who want to visit.
