Rowy

LoMit, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Seaside town with traditional wooden houses and shops in the Pomeranian Voivodeship has sandy beaches and is close to Gardno Lake for water sports. It's also near Słowiński National Park, home to sand dunes, forests, and lakes.
