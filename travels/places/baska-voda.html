Baska Voda

Dreizung, Public domain, via Wikimedia Commons

Popular resort on the Makarska riviera with a selection of beaches to choose from and hiking trails. When you are ready for some time away from the sun, there's an archaeological museum and malacological museum (sea shell collection...)
