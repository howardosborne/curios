Benicassim

Martin Cox from Benicasim, España, CC BY 2.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Benicàssim is a municipality and beach resort located in the province of Castelló, on the Costa del Azahar. The Desert de les Palmes mountain range further inland shelters the town from the north wind.
