Pietrasanta

Davide Papalini, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Renowned for its marble, famous painters and sculptors, including the Colombian Fernando Botero, have put down roots here, making it an open-air contemporary art museum. 3 km inland from the coast, one of a string of places good for cycling.
