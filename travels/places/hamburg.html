Hamburg

NielsFahrenkrog, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Hamburg (like Lubeck) has a long and proudly independent history as free city that had been part of the Hanseatic League (HH on the number plates refers to Hansa Hamburg).

There's loads to see and do, or if you prefer, you can just do nothing and pass the time loafing around the string of parks from Planten und Blumen down to the Elbe or stroll around the Alster.

Architecture is a big thing here, with the Chilehaus among the many highlights. 

Hamburg Greeters

If you want to be shown around the city by a local, why not book a greeter for free? Details can be found here: https://www.hamburg-greeter.de/
