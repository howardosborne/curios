Dresden

User:Kolossos, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

The city of Dresden was heavily bombed during World War II, but has been beautifully restored to its former glory. You can admire the stunning Baroque architecture of the Zwinger Palace, the Semper Opera House and the Frauenkirche, as well as explore the rich collections of art and history in the museums.
