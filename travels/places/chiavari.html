Chiavari

Dapa19, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Ligurian coastal town with a beachside promenade and a marina and is situated near the river Entella. It's known for its beautiful historic center, picturesque harbor, and sandy beaches. The heart of Chiavari is the Centro Storico, a maze of narrow streets lined with colorful palazzos, arcaded shops, and elegant cafes. Be sure to visit the Piazza Matteotti, the main square, which is home to the beautiful Chiavari Cathedral (Cattedrale di Nostra Signora dell'Orto).
