Orte

LigaDue, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Town overlooking Tiber valley where the famous Palio degli Arcieri takes place. The Basilica of Santa Maria Assunta, the Church of San Pietro and the Church of San Biagio, as well as the Diocesan Museum of Orte, the Municipal Archaeological Museum, and the Museum of the Confraternities are worth a visit.
