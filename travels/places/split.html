Split

Tatyana Peshkova, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Split is a beautiful city with a lot to offer visitors. Diocletian's Palace is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most popular tourist attractions in Croatia. Visitors can also visit the Riva waterfront, a lively promenade with shops, restaurants, and bars, and Marjan Hill, a hill with stunning views of the city and the Adriatic Sea. Split is also a great place to enjoy Croatian cuisine and culture.
