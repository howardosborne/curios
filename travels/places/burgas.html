Burgas

Evgord, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Burgas is another coastal city that offers a relaxing seaside escape. It has a beautiful beach, a spacious park, and a modern city centre. Burgas is also a gateway to some of the best natural wonders of Bulgaria, such as the Rila Lakes, the Strandzha Mountain, and the Pomorie Lake.
