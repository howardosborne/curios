Peschici

Creator-bz, CC BY-SA 1.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Peschici is a town and comune in the province of Foggia in the Apulia region of southeast Italy. Famous for its seaside resorts, its territory belongs to the Gargano National Park and to the Gargano Mountain Community.
