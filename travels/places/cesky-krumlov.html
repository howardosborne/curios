Cesky Krumlov

Xth-Floor, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Cesky Krumlov is a picturesque town in the south of the country, surrounded by a meandering river and a medieval castle. The town has a charming atmosphere, with cobblestone streets, colorful houses, and cozy cafes.
