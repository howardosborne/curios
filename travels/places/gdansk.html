Gdansk

Diego Delso, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Gdańsk is a beautiful city with a lot to offer visitors. The Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is home to many well-preserved medieval buildings. Visitors can also visit Mariacka Street, a charming street lined with shops and restaurants, and St. Mary's Church, a beautiful Gothic church.
