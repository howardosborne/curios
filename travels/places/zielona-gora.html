Zielona Góra

LZG, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Known as the The City of Wine around Poland due to it being one of the few places that can produce a fair bottle or two. The name Zielona Góra actually means Green Mountain and the famous Palm House on Wine Hill is popular with tourists.
