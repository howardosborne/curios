Schleswig

Christian Knoke

Christian Knoke

Town in northern Germant with a distinctly scandinavian feel. Capitolplatz is a lively square in the heart of Schleswig with a variety of shops, restaurants, and cafes. Große Freiheit is a pedestrian street with a variety of shops, restaurants, and cafes. Schleswig Cathedral is a Romanesque cathedral dating back to the 12th century. Further out, the historic fishing village of Holm has a charming harbor and traditional houses. Gottorf Castle is a 16th century castle on an island housing national art & archaeology museums, plus a restaurant.
