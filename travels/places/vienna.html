Vienna

© Jorge Royan / http://www.royan.com.ar

The capital of Austria is a must-see for any visitor. Vienna has something for everyone, from art and music to architecture and cuisine. You can admire the majestic Schönbrunn Palace, the former residence of the Habsburgs, or visit the Belvedere Palace, which houses the largest collection of Gustav Klimt's paintings. You can also enjoy the green spaces of Vienna, such as the Prater park or the Gardens of Mirabell Palace.
