Krakow

Maatex, CC BY-SA 3.0 PL , via Wikimedia Commons

Kraków is a charming city with a lot to offer visitors. The Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is home to many well-preserved medieval buildings, including the Wawel Royal Castle and the Main Market Square. Kraków is also a great place to enjoy Polish cuisine and culture.
