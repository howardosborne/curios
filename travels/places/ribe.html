Ribe

Hjart, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Denmark's oldest town, with a well-preserved medieval center. Visitors can visit the Ribe Cathedral, the oldest cathedral in Denmark, and the Ribe Viking Centre, an open-air museum that recreates Viking life.
