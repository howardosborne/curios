Subotica

Mister No, CC BY 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

City in the very north of Vojvodina in northern Serbia which thanks to its complicated past is well known for its many great examples of Art Nouveau;. The multicultural (Hungarian/Serbian/Croatian) city has a lively student scene, as well as many festivals.
