Delft

Herebedug, Public domain, via Wikimedia Commons

Delft is a picturesque town that is famous for its blue-and-white pottery and its association with the painter Johannes Vermeer. It has a lovely historic centre with canals, bridges, and churches, as well as a lively market square and some interesting museums. You can reach Delft by train from Amsterdam or The Hague in less than an hour.
