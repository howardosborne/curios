Auschwitz-Birkenau

Lukaszmt, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

The Auschwitz-Birkenau Memorial and Museum is a sobering but important place to visit. Admission is free, but a guided tour is highly recommended. General guided tours last around 3.5 hours and cover both Auschwitz I and Birkenau sites.
