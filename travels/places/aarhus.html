Aarhus

Zairon, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Denmark's second-largest city and a vibrant cultural hub. Visitors can enjoy Aarhus' many museums, including the ARoS Aarhus Art Museum and the Moesgaard Museum, as well as its lively waterfront area.
