Linkoping

tomhe, CC BY 2.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

A city in southern Sweden, known for its cathedral and air force museum. The city has the advantage of being a major city with all that it entails yet retaining the cozy quiet small town feel, largely due to the lack of tall buildings; a result of the military airfield just outside the city.
