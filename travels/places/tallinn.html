Tallinn

Dennis Jarvis, CC BY-SA 2.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Tallinn is a charming city with a lot to offer visitors. The Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is home to many well-preserved medieval buildings. Visitors can also visit Toompea Castle, the seat of the Estonian Parliament, and Alexander Nevsky Cathedral, one of the largest Orthodox cathedrals in Northern Europe. Tallinn is also a great place to enjoy Estonian cuisine and culture.
