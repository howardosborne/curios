Rotterdam

Mlefter, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

The second-largest city in the Netherlands, Rotterdam is a hub of innovation and creativity. It is known for its futuristic architecture, such as the Erasmus Bridge and the Cube Houses, as well as its cultural diversity and lively nightlife. You can take a direct train from Amsterdam to Rotterdam in less than an hour.
