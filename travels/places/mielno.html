Mielno

Petrb, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Popular tourist destination with sandy beaches on the Baltic Sea coast. It lies on a spit between the sea and the large Jamno lake. While it's popular with all kinds of tourists in the summer, during the winter a particular kind gather around the Pomnik Morsa w Mielnie (Monument of the Walrus) - winter bathing enthusiasts.
