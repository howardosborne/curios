Telc

Michal Lewi, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Another UNESCO World Heritage Site, Telc is a small town in Southern Moravia that looks like a fairy tale. Telc has a stunning main square, lined with colorful Renaissance houses with arcades and gables. You can also visit the Telc Castle, a Renaissance masterpiece with lavish interiors and gardens. You can take a direct train from Prague to Telc in about three hours.
