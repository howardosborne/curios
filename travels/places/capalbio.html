Capalbio

Patafisik, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Town located between the Tyrrhenian Sea and the hilly hinterland. The village is dominated by its castle of the same name, which Charlemagne donated to the Abbey of Saints Anastasius and Vincent alle Tre Fontane in 805.
