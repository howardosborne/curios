Lagos

Oleksii.Mykus, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

The Algarve is a popular tourist destination for its beautiful beaches, dramatic coastline, and golf courses.The walled old town offers a glimpse into Lagos' past. Explore its cobbled streets, fortified walls, and historical landmarks like the Igreja de Santo António church and the Castelo dos Governadores castle.
