Ferrol

Lmbuga via Wikimedia Commons

City on the Atlantic coast best known for its easter celebrations. Ferrol old town contains the Modernist La Magdalena neighbourhood, the La Cortina defensive walls (18th century), and the castles of San Felipe and La Palma.
