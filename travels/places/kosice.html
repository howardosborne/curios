Kosice

Maros M r a z (Maros), CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Košice is a beautiful city with a rich history. The Old Town is home to many well-preserved medieval buildings, including St. Elisabeth's Cathedral, a Gothic cathedral that is one of the largest churches in Slovakia. Visitors can also visit the Singing Fountain, a musical fountain that puts on a show every evening. Košice is also a great place to enjoy Slovak cuisine and culture.
