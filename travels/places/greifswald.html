Greifswald

User:Axt, Public domain, via Wikimedia Commons

University and Hanseatic city with striking buildings, particularly the University of Greifswald. The towers of the three distinctive churches are called Long Nicholas, Fat Marie and Little Jacob by the locals. A worthwhile museum for art lovers is the Pommersche Landesmuseum Greifswald. Here you will find, among other things, original works by the Greifswald-born artist Caspar David Friedrichs. In his honor, there are also exhibitions in his residence, today's Caspar David Friedrich Center. Music festivals, such as the Eldena Jazz Evenings, Greifswalder Bachwoche, the fishing festival Gaffelrigg or the Nordic Sound already have a long tradition.
