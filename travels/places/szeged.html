Szeged

Kozma János, Public domain, via Wikimedia Commons

The third-largest city in Hungary is located on the banks of the Tisza river in the south-east of the country. It is known for its sunny climate, its paprika production, and its festivals. You can visit the Votive Church, the Synagogue, and the Pick Salami Museum.
