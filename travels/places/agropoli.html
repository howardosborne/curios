Agropoli

RealNordyNed, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Agropoli is a town in Campania situated at the start of the Cilentan Coast, on the Tyrrhenian Sea. Agropoli is known for its beautiful beaches, its historic center, and its mild climate. Things to do include visiting Castello Aragonese, built in the 12th century, and now a museum that houses a collection of artifacts from the town's history.
