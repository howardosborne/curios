Budapest

Jorge Franganillo from Barcelona, Spain, CC BY 2.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

The capital of Hungary is a must-see for any visitor. It has stunning architecture, vibrant nightlife, and famous thermal baths. You can explore the Castle Hill, the Parliament Building, and the Chain Bridge along the Danube river.
