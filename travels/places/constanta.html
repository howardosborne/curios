Constanta

Leontin l, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

The largest port and seaside resort on the Black Sea coast, Constanta is a popular destination for sunbathing, swimming, and partying. You can also visit the ancient ruins of Tomis, the Casino, and the Aquarium.
