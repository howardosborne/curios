Spa

Jean-Pol GRANDMONT, CC BY 2.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Spa is a town in the Ardennes region of eastern Belgium. It's known for its mineral-rich thermal waters. In the Villa Royale, the Musée de la Ville d’Eaux features decorative wooden boxes and exhibits about the town's history as a spa destination. The stone Church of Notre Dame and St. Remacle was built in Rhenish-Romanesque style. In the southeast, surrounded by forest, is the Spa-Francorchamps motor-racing circuit.
