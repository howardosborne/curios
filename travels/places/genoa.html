Genoa

Dapa19, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

A port city with a rich maritime history, Genoa is known for its narrow streets, vibrant atmosphere, and delicious pesto sauce. Visit the Aquarium of Genoa, the Doge's Palace, and the Cathedral of San Lorenzo, and don't forget to try some of the city's famous focaccia and pasta al pesto.
