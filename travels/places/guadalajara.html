Guadalajara

Juan Carlos Castle, CC BY-SA 3.0 ES , via Wikimedia Commons

City with a rich history leaving architectural legacy in its old town. The City Hall, the great houses of the Plaza de los Dávalos or the old palace of the Mendozas, whose internal courtyard is an excellent example of Platersque art. But the most important civil building in the capital of La Alcarria is the Infantado Palace, considered a real symbol of the city.
