Sarzana

via Wikimedia Commons

Sarzana is a hiddlen gem nestled between the Liguirian coast and the Apuan Alps. It boasts a rich history dating back to Roman times and has a well preserved medieval centre and an impressive 15th-century fortress which overlooks the town and surrounding countryside. The Porta Romana / Roman Gate can be found at the crossroads of the Via Aurelia and the Via Francigena, an ancient pigrimate rounte from Canterbury to Rome.
