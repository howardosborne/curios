Groningen

Bert Kaufmann, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Groningen is a lively city that is located in the north of the Netherlands. It is known for its youthful vibe and its cultural scene. It has many attractions for visitors, such as the Groninger Museum, the Martini Tower, and the Prinsentuin Park. You can reach Groningen by train from Amsterdam in about 2 hours.
