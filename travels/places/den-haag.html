Den Haag

Zairon, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

The seat of the Dutch government and the home of the royal family, The Hague is a city of elegance and sophistication. It has many impressive buildings, such as the Binnenhof and the Peace Palace, as well as some of the finest art museums in the country, such as the Mauritshuis and the Gemeentemuseum. You can travel from Amsterdam to The Hague by train in about an hour.
