Montélimar

Gachepi, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Famous for its nougat, Montélimar is a town in the Drôme department in the Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes region in Southeastern France. It is the second-largest town in the department after Valence.
