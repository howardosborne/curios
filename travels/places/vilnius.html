Vilnius

BigHead, CC BY-SA 4.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Vilnius is a charming city with a lot to offer visitors. The Old Town is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is home to many well-preserved medieval buildings. Visitors can also visit Gediminas' Tower, the highest point in the city, and Trakai Castle, a beautiful island castle located just outside the city. Vilnius is also a great place to enjoy Lithuanian cuisine and culture.
