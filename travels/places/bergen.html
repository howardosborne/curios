Bergen

TomasEE, CC BY 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Bergen is a charming city with a lot to offer visitors. The city center is compact and easy to explore on foot, and there are many museums, galleries, and shops to visit. Bergen is also a great base for exploring the surrounding fjords and mountains. Visitors can take a boat trip to the nearby Nærøyfjord or Aurlandsfjord, or go hiking in the Bergen Mountains.
