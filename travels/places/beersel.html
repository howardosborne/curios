Beersel

Alessia Tavcar, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Town on the edge of Brussels with the best preserved medieval castle in the country and home of geuze beer. Beersel also has two traditional, authentic geuze breweries, Oud Beersel and 3 Fonteinen. People like to eat boterham met plattekaas en radijzen (sandwich with white cheese and radishes) with their beer. and will also stretch to some mandjeskaas (literally 'basket cheese'), which is a white cheese stored in little baskets.
