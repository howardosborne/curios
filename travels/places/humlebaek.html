Humlebaek

Urbandweller, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Just a short train ride from Copenhagen, Humblebaek is home of the world-renowned Louisiana Museum of Modern Art, the most visited museum in Denmark, which has an extensive permanent collection as well as top-class temporary exhibitions.
