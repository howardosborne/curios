Ljubljana

Ville Miettinen, CC BY 2.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Ljubljana is a charming city with a lot to offer visitors. The Old Town is home to many well-preserved medieval buildings, including Ljubljana Castle, a castle that dates back to the 12th century. Visitors can also visit the Dragon Bridge, a famous bridge decorated with dragon statues. Ljubljana is also a great place to enjoy Slovenian cuisine and culture.
