Sintra

Singa Hitam, CC BY 2.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Sintra is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is home to many well-preserved castles and palaces, including the Pena Palace, a colorful castle that sits on top of a hill, and the Quinta da Regaleira, a mysterious estate with gardens, grottoes, and tunnels. Sintra is also a great place to go hiking and enjoy the stunning scenery.
