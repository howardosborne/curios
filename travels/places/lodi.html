Lodi

Zuffe, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

City and comune in Lombardy, northern Italy, primarily on the western bank of the River Adda. Piazza della Vittoria, listed by the Italian Touring Club among the most beautiful squares in Italy. Featuring porticoes on all its four sides, it includes the Basilica della Vergine Assunta and the Broletto (town hall).
