Halle (Saale)

Fenchelkiwi1, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Town that became synonymous with salt production but was also the birthplace of Bach and Handel. There are museums to all three. You can also see the oldest representation of the cosmos - The Nebra Sky Disc at the museum of Prehistory.
