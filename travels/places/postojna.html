Postojna

Shadowgate from Novara, ITALY, CC BY 2.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Postojna Cave is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Slovenia. Visitors can take a guided tour of the cave and see its many stalagmites, stalactites, and other cave formations. They can also take a cave train ride, which takes them through the cave system.
