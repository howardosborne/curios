Bogliasco

Sidvics, CC BY-SA 3.0 , via Wikimedia Commons

Town in Liguria located about 11 kilometres southeast of Genoa. Together with the comuni of Camogli, Recco, Pieve Ligure and Sori, it is part of the so-called Golfo Paradiso. Economy is mostly based on tourism; agriculture include production of olives.
